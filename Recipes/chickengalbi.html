Chicken Galbi Meal Prep

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken theigh (can also use breast)
  • 2 tbsp of Korean galbi sauce
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Frozen peas & corn // Butter, Salt, Pepper, Minced Garlic
  • 3 cups of uncoooked rice
  • Rice seasoning (furikake)
  • Soy sauce

Optional Ingredients: Eggs, dried onion.

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Cut the chicken and place into a bowl along with the Korean galbi sauce & black pepper. Marinate for 30 minutes
  3. Cook peas & corn inside a pot alongisde the butter, salt, pepper, and minced garlic.
  4. If desired, can also scamble 5 eggs. Set aside.
  5. Once chicken is done marinating, pan fry on high 3/4 minutes on each side.
  6. Equally distribute the rice, peas, corn, and egg into 4 separate containers.
  7. Season the rice with furikake and soy sauce.
  8. Once chicken is cooked, distirubte equally between the containers.
  9. Top with dried onion if desired.
